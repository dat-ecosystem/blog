Code for Science & Society Community Call - May 31, 11am PST

We've been traveling so much that we are a little late on the announcement (eek it's next Thursday!) but please mark your calendars for the next Code for Science & Society Quarterly Community Call.

Thursday, May 31, 2018: 11am Pacific / 2pm Eastern / 7pm UK / 8pm Berlin / June 1, 6am NZ

We have an incredible line up of speakers:

  • Yoshua Wuyts
  • Naomi Penfold, PhD
  • Hugh Isaacs II
  • Georgia Bullen / Measurement Lab
  • Mathias Buus Madsen / Dat & Beaker
  • Nokome Bentley / Stencila
  • Rik Smith-Una / ScienceFair

The last one was super fun, so you won't want to miss this.

How do you watch? Thanks to Aurelia Moser at Mozilla, you can tune in on Air Mozilla and ask live questions on our Etherpad.

To recap:

  • May 31, 2018: 11am Pacific / 2pm Eastern / 7pm UK / 8pm Berlin / June 1, 6am NZ
  • Read along, add your updates, and ask questions here: https://public.etherpad-mozilla.org/p/CSS-community-call-May-31-2018
  • Watch the call here: https://air.mozilla.org/mozilla-science-lab-may-2018-bi-monthly-community-call/
  • Can we feature your work on an upcoming call? You better believe we have a spreadsheet going, so email us hi@codeforscience.org to get on a future call.
