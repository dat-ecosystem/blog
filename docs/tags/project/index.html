Unveiling the HOP (Health Oracle Protocol)

HOP (Health Oracle Protocol) is an open-source coherence with a non-coding graphical interface toolkit called Bento Box. It empowers individuals to control and understand the data shaping health, communities, and nature, democratizing access to data tools while preserving privacy. Join the cosmic journey of HOP as we reshape the future of peer-to-peer technology and unlock the transformative power of connectedness.

The Decentralized Streaming Adventure with Sher!

Sher is a decentralized streaming platform for content creators and streamers, powered by P2P technology and focused on community building. With a vision to revolutionize the entertainment galaxy, Sher offers a resilient streaming experience, supports podcasters in monetizing their content, and aims to create a vibrant community where cosmic companions can collaborate and thrive.

Interview

Meet Mauve and their Agregore Browser

Get ready to explore a local-first web browser that targets off-grid communities and embraces decentralized protocols - meet Mauve, the founder of Agregore, an innovative web browser that uses peer-to-peer protocols for local-first web apps, offering a sustainable and secure alternative to corporate browsers. Agregore is just the start of Mauve's vision for local-first software, which focuses on addressing social issues and promoting sustainability and resilience in the digital world.
