Decentralized Galaxy of Writing with Hans Degroote

Hyperpubee is a decentralized publishing platform created by Hans Degroote, where writers can share their work peer-to-peer, remixing and collaborating with fellow wordsmiths. With an open-source code available on Gitlab and innovative features like music integration, Hyperpubee offers a cosmic playground for writers and journalists seeking to explore new frontiers in the world of decentralized publishing.

Exploring Maori Community Archiving with Mix Irving

Delve into the world of Maori community archiving with Mix Irving from Ahau, as we uncover the transformative power of peer-to-peer software in preserving family trees and captivating stories. Through grants and partnerships with Maori collectives, this project empowers communities to shape their narratives and rediscover their ancestral roots in the digital age.

Unveiling the HOP (Health Oracle Protocol)

HOP (Health Oracle Protocol) is an open-source coherence with a non-coding graphical interface toolkit called Bento Box. It empowers individuals to control and understand the data shaping health, communities, and nature, democratizing access to data tools while preserving privacy. Join the cosmic journey of HOP as we reshape the future of peer-to-peer technology and unlock the transformative power of connectedness.
